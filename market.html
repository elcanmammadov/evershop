Market Sectors

EVERSHOP delivers specialized fabrication, engineering, and supply solutions to the world's most demanding industries. Our expertise spans Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power Generation, and Water Treatment sectors, where quality, safety, and reliability are non-negotiable.

Oil & Gas

Pressure vessels, separators, heat exchangers, and process equipment for upstream, midstream, and downstream operations.

Petrochemical

Specialized reactors, columns, and process skids designed for corrosive and high-temperature chemical processing environments.

Power Generation

Boilers, heat recovery steam generators, condensers, and auxiliary equipment for thermal and combined-cycle power plants.

Water Treatment

Complete skid packages, filtration systems, and specialized vessels for industrial water treatment and desalination facilities.

Industrial Manufacturing

Custom fabrication solutions including structural steel, process equipment, and specialized components for diverse manufacturing applications.

Our Core Capabilities Across Sectors

Regardless of industry, EVERSHOP delivers consistent engineering excellence through our integrated approach.

ASME Code Compliance

All pressure vessels and heat exchangers fabricated to ASME Section VIII Division 1 & 2 standards, with full documentation and U-stamp certification where required.

Advanced Materials Engineering

Expertise in exotic alloys (Inconel, Monel, Hastelloy), duplex/super duplex stainless steels, and specialized cladding for corrosive service environments.

Integrated Project Management

End-to-end coordination from design and material sourcing through fabrication, testing, and delivery—ensuring on-time, on-budget project completion.

Quality Assurance & Testing

Comprehensive QA/QC programs including NDT (RT, UT, MT, PT), hydrostatic testing, material traceability, and full inspection documentation.

Local Manufacturing with Global Standards

Made in Azerbaijan facility combining local production efficiency with international engineering standards and global component sourcing capabilities.
