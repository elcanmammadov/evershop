Raw Materials & Pre-Fabricated Supply

Global Sourcing. Certified Quality. Project Ready.

As a single-source partner, EVERSHOP manages the critical supply chain for the Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, and Water Treatment industries. We specialize in sourcing hard-to-find materials and providing pre-fabricated components that meet the exact technical requirements of your project.

Strategic Raw Material Sourcing

We leverage an international network (USA, EU, UK, Japan, South Korea) to supply high-grade structural and process materials with full mill traceability and certification.

Pre-Fabricated Components

Reduce onsite assembly time with cut-to-size steel plates, beveled piping sections, and pre-assembled structural sub-sections prepared to your exact specifications.

Specialized Alloys for Harsh Environments

Expert procurement of high-performance metals: Stainless & Duplex Steels (300/400 Series, 904L), and exotic alloys including Nickel, Inconel, Monel, Hastelloy, and Titanium.

Forged & Formed Materials

Supply of high-integrity forged components, rings, and shafts. Hot and cold formed components from 1/4" up to 54" for water treatment and petrochemical piping networks.

NACE & Project-Specific Compliance

All materials supplied according to international standards (ASTM, ASME, DIN, GOST) and can meet NACE MR0175/ISO 15156 requirements for sour service environments.
