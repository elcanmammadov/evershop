Our Story

EVERSHOP was established in 2018 by a group of professional engineers driven by a singular vision:

"To support local industrial growth through engineering and value-added products"

Our founders brought with them a vast expertise across three critical pillars:

Our Expertise: Three Critical Pillars

Manufacturing

A deep understanding of metallurgy, high-precision fabrication, and international engineering standards (ASME, EN, ГОСТ, API).

Procurement

Decades of experience in global strategic sourcing, ensuring that the right materials reach the right projects without compromise.

Project Management

A proven track record of delivering complex industrial projects on time, within budget, and to the highest safety specifications.

The Single-Source Partner
